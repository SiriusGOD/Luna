One-click ride-hailing

  • ◎ Description:
    "QR code scanning to call a car" is provided at the entrances of stations, restaurants, and business offices to provide scanning and calling services for passengers who have not downloaded the "Taxi Calling App" to call a car in time.

    In order to provide corporate image design for each corporate building, ColorStyle.css of various color systems is provided for corporates and stores to choose from.

    Develop two versions, use AB Text to test the difference in the user's response to version A and version B, and then judge which method A or B is better. There are two layouts, one is a more traditional style (designed by the marketing design department), and the other is a more modern style (designed by myself).
  • ◎ Use tools:
    Figma, HTML, SCSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, RWD, Lottie Animations
